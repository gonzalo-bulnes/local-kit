Ever wondered how a personal ID number looks like in Chile?

And in Thailand, Ireland or Senegal? Or how to validate them? How to do business there?

Every day, we use software which solves geographically, culturally, let's say locally-specific problems (which include performing national payments online or getting financial indicators). From time to time we write that software.

Local Kit makes easy to share locally-specific software, to find it and to get in touch with skilled developers wherever you go.

Who are we? How to contribute?

We are designers, developers, we love our work, we believe diverse, open-minded and skilled individuals make better teams, better companies and better software together.

We love open-source, and are glad you're thinking to contribute. It's easy by the way, get in touch with Local Kit contributors near of you, or fork the Local Kit repository on Github , add your own Local Kit to the Active Local Kits list and open a pull-request.

Use it in Chile,
reproduce it everywhere
